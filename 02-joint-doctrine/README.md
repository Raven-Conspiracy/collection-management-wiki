# Joint Doctrine

_Joint Doctrine › Index_

---

The keystone and capstone publications governing US joint intelligence collection. Each page below summarizes a publication, extracts its key terms, and shows how its concepts cross-reference into the rest of the collection management process.

## Pages

- [JP 2-0 — Joint Intelligence](jp-2-0-joint-intelligence.md)
  - The keystone for joint intelligence. Defines the intelligence process, intelligence disciplines, CRM/COM, Collection Management Authority, and the role of the J-2.
- [JP 2-01 — Joint and National Intelligence Support to Military Operations](jp-2-01-joint-and-national-intel-support.md)
  - The operational handbook. Owns the collection management chapter, JCMB, JIPCL, requirements hierarchy, NAI/TAI, indicators, signatures, LTIOV, persistent surveillance, tipping and cueing, PED/TPED/TCPED, and national-to-tactical integration.
- [JP 3-0 — Joint Campaigns and Operations](jp-3-0-joint-campaigns-and-operations.md)
  - Frames ISR within joint operations across all phases. Source of CCIR/PIR/FFIR construct.
- [JP 3-60 — Joint Targeting](jp-3-60-joint-targeting.md)
  - The six-phase joint targeting cycle and how intelligence collection feeds (and gets fed by) targeting at every phase. Owns F2T2EA for dynamic targeting.
