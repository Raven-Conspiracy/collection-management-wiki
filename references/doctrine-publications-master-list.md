## Key Collection Management Terms: Quick Reference Glossary

| Term | Doctrinal Definition | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **CMA** | Authority to establish, prioritize, and validate theater collection requirements, establish sensor tasking guidance, and develop theater-wide collection policies | JP 2-01 |
| **CRM** | Authoritative development and control of collection, processing, exploitation, and information reporting requirements | JP 2-0 / JP 2-01 |
| **COM** | Authoritative direction, scheduling, and control of specific collection operations and associated processing, exploitation, and information reporting resources | JP 2-0 / JP 2-01 |
| **PIR** | Intelligence requirement stated as a priority for intelligence support that the commander and staff need to understand the adversary or operational environment | JP 2-0 |
| **EEI** | Most critical information requirements regarding the adversary and the environment needed by the commander by a particular time | JP 2-0 |
| **SIR** | A basic question that must be answered to satisfy a collection request (JP 2-0); a discrete piece of information answering an indicator (ATP 2-01) | JP 2-0 / ATP 2-01 |
| **RFI** | Specific, time-sensitive, ad hoc requirement for information or intelligence products; distinct from standing requirements | JP 2-0 |
| **CCIR** | Information requirement identified by the commander as critical to facilitating timely decision-making; includes PIRs and FFIRs | JP 3-0 |
| **JIPCL** | Joint Integrated Prioritized Collection List; all validated collection requirements at CCMD level; basis for national tasking | JP 2-01 |
| **JCMB** | Joint Collection Management Board; chaired by J-2; forum for collection management across joint force; approves JIPCL | JP 2-01 |
| **JCWG** | Joint Collection Working Group; working-level forum for synchronizing and prioritizing collection; feeds JCMB | AFDP 2-0 |
| **LTIOV** | Latest Time Information is of Value; deadline by which collected information must arrive to be useful | JP 2-01 |
| **NAI** | Named Area of Interest; geographic area where information satisfying a specific IR can be collected | ATP 2-01.3 |
| **TAI** | Target Area of Interest; geographic area associated with high-value targets | ATP 2-01.3 |
| **ISR** | Activity that synchronizes and integrates the planning and operation of sensors, assets, and PED systems in direct support of operations | JP 2-0 |
| **PED/TPED/TCPED** | Processing, Exploitation, Dissemination / Tasking-PED / Tasking-Collection-PED; the downstream activities converting raw collection into finished intelligence | JP 2-01 |
| **NIPF** | National Intelligence Priorities Framework; presidential-directed system for prioritizing national intelligence activities | ICD 204 |
| **JIPOE** | Joint Intelligence Preparation of the Operational Environment; continuous J-2-managed process analyzing the OE and adversary | JP 2-0 |
| **JIOC** | Joint Intelligence Operations Center; CCMD focal point for intelligence planning, CMA, analysis, and production | JP 2-01 / NWP 2-01 |
| **JISE** | Joint Intelligence Support Element; subordinate JTF intelligence element providing continuous analytical capability | JP 2-01 |
| **NIST** | National Intelligence Support Team; DIA-led team deploying national IC capability to JTF J-2 | JP 2-01 / NWP 2-01 |
| **ISRD** | ISR Division of the AOC; central node for Air Force collection management; conducts COM for theater airborne ISR | AFDP 2-0 |
| **MIOC** | Maritime Intelligence Operations Center; all-source intel org embedded in MOC; Navy equivalent of JIOC at operational level | NWP 2-01 |
| **CMDO** | Collection Management and Dissemination Officer (USMC); formulates ICRs and IDRs; tasks organic/supporting collection | MCWP 2-6 |
| **DCGS** | Distributed Common Ground System; Service-specific PED systems providing theater-to-national ISR exploitation | AFDP 2-0 / GDIT |
| **Tipping & Cueing** | Use of one intel discipline/asset to cross-cue collection by a more precise sensor | JP 2-01 |
| **Persistent Surveillance** | Collection strategy providing near-continuous, all-weather, day/night surveillance of high-value targets | JP 2-01 |
