# Case Studies

_Case Studies › Index_

---

> **Status:** Open for contribution.

Worked examples of collection management in practice. **Unclassified, open-source, or declassified material only.** Strong preference for material that has been written about publicly in CRS, GAO, official histories, or major journalism.

## Suggested Case Studies

- **OPERATION DESERT STORM (1990–91)** — joint ISR fusion in the deep battle; collection management lessons on theater-strategic ISR before the modern DCGS era.
- **OEF / OIF dynamic targeting** — F3EAD evolution; persistent ISR and the rise of the MQ-9/MQ-1 enterprise; PED scaling crises.
- **Defeat of UBL (2011, public account)** — multi-INT fusion against a single high-value target over years.
- **Russia–Ukraine war (2022–present)** — commercial space ISR integration, lessons on contested-space collection, near-peer EW environment.
- **JSTARS retirement transition** — GMTI capability gap and substitution strategy.
- **AFRICOM persistent ISR** — long-dwell low-density environments; reachback PED challenges.

Each case study page should follow this structure:

1. Operational context and the commander's PIRs
2. Collection architecture (platforms, PED, authorities)
3. What worked
4. What broke
5. Doctrinal lessons (tied to JP / FM / AFDP / NWP / MCWP)
6. References (open-source only)
