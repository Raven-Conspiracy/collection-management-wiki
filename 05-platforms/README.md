## Editorial Notes

- Platform performance envelopes (endurance, ceiling, range) are public-domain best estimates. Real-world envelopes vary by configuration, payload, and operational profile.
- Sensor designations are at the public-disclosure level. Specific signal/signature library content is **not** in scope of this wiki.
- Retired platforms (JSTARS, EP-3E, MC-12W) are retained for doctrinal continuity and historical reference; current substitutes are noted in each entry.
- Emerging programs (ARTEMIS, ARES, HADES, E-7 Wedgetail US fielding) are tracked in their respective entries with current program status.
