## 6. MASINT — Airborne Platforms

### 6.1 RC-135S Cobra Ball

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Designation** | RC-135S Cobra Ball (3 aircraft — numbers vary by modification cycle) |
| **Operator** | USAF (45th Reconnaissance Squadron, Offutt AFB; deploys globally on short notice) |
| **Type** | Manned National-Level MASINT / Ballistic Missile Intelligence Aircraft |
| **Collection Capability** | **MASINT Primary:** MIRA (Medium Wave Infrared Array) — long-range IR sensor for tracking ballistic missile boost/midcourse/reentry phases; RTOS (Real Time Optics System) — wide-field acquisition staring array; LATS (Large Aperture Tracker System) — precision narrow-field tracker; All-weather tracking radar; SIGINT sensors for missile-associated signals (FISINT/TELINT); ballistic signature collection for treaty verification |
| **Sensor Names** | MIRA IR array; RTOS staring sensor; LATS precision optical tracker; all-weather tracking radar; SIGINT telemetry collection suite |
| **Performance Envelope** | Same C-135 airframe; aerial refueling capable (unlimited range); can deploy anywhere globally in 24 hours; Crew: 2 pilots, 1 navigator, 3 EWOs, 2 airborne systems engineers, 2+ airborne mission specialists |
| **Datalinks / Comms** | SATCOM; HF; dedicated national intelligence data links |
| **PED Architecture** | National MASINT consumers: DIA (MSIC); NSA; DTRA; NCA; missile defense community |
| **Tasking Authority** | JCS-directed (national priority); SECDEF authorization; STRATCOM |
| **Primary Mission Set** | Optical and electronic data collection on ballistic missile tests; arms treaty compliance verification (START, INF legacy); characterization of foreign missile capabilities; THAAD/NMD threat assessment inputs; theater ballistic missile proliferation monitoring |
| **Limitations** | 3-aircraft (limited fleet size — high-demand/low-density); must be in theater proximity for collection; classified positioning requirements for each mission |
| **Program Status** | Active national asset; continuously modernized MASINT suite |
| **Public Sources** | [AF Fact Sheet Cobra Ball](https://www.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/104498/rc-135s-cobra-ball/) | [FAS Cobra Ball](https://irp.fas.org/program/collect/cobra_ball.htm) | [Air & Space Forces](https://www.airandspaceforces.com/weapons-platforms/rc-135s/) |
