## 1. Intelligence Discipline Overview & TCPED Framework

| INT Discipline | Definition | Primary DoD Collection Agencies |
|---|---|---|
| **IMINT** | Imagery Intelligence — EO, IR, SAR still/video imagery | NGA, NRO, USAF, USN, USA |
| **GEOINT** | Geospatial Intelligence — IMINT + geospatial data fusion | NGA |
| **SIGINT/COMINT** | Communications Intelligence — comms exploitation | NSA/CSS, USAF, USA |
| **SIGINT/ELINT** | Electronic Intelligence — non-comms emitter exploitation | NSA/CSS, USAF, USN |
| **MASINT** | Measurement & Signature Intelligence — phenomenological | DIA/MSIC, USAF, NRO |
| **ACINT** | Acoustic Intelligence — subset of MASINT | USN, IUSS |
| **HUMINT** | Human Intelligence — human source collection | DIA/DCS, CIA, USMC MISO/CI |
| **OSINT** | Open Source Intelligence — publicly available | All services, NGA |
| **CYBINT/DNINT** | Cyber/Digital Network Intelligence | NSA/CSS, CYBERCOM CMF |

**TCPED Chain:** Tasking → Collection → Processing → Exploitation → Dissemination

## 2. DoD UAS Group Classification Reference

Per the DoD UAS Roadmap and Army UAS Roadmap 2010–2035:

| Group | Max GTOW (lb) | Normal Operating Altitude | Airspeed (kts) | Representative Systems |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | 0–20 | <1,200 ft AGL | <100 | RQ-11B Raven, RQ-12A Wasp, RQ-20A Puma |
| **2** | 21–55 | <3,500 ft MSL | <250 | RQ-20B Puma AE Enhanced, small ship-launched UAS |
| **3** | <1,320 | <18,000 ft MSL | <250 | RQ-7B Shadow, RQ-21A Blackjack, JUMP-20, V-BAT |
| **4** | >1,320 | <18,000 ft MSL | Any | MQ-1C Gray Eagle, MQ-8C Fire Scout |
