# IC Matrix Template (Army, ATP 2-01)

_Templates › Plans_

---

The **Information Collection Matrix** is the Army's primary collection-planning tool, defined in [ATP 2-01](https://armypubs.army.mil/). It pairs with the IC Overlay (geographic view) and IC Plan (Annex L of the OPORD).

## Canonical Column Structure (ATP 2-01)

| # | Column | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | PIR / IR | Parent requirement |
| 2 | Indicator | What you're looking for |
| 3 | SIR | Specific Information Requirement |
| 4 | NAI / TAI | Geographic area |
| 5 | LTIOV | Latest time information is of value |
| 6 | Asset | Tasked collector |
| 7 | Reports To | PED / reporting destination |
| 8 | Reporting Time | When the report is required |
| 9 | Remarks | Cueing, redundancy, threat notes |

## Companion Products

- **IC Overlay** — geographic depiction of NAIs/TAIs and asset coverage
- **IC Plan** — narrative plan, typically Annex L of the OPORD
- **IC Synchronization** — time-phased version of the IC Matrix, similar to joint ISR Sync Matrix
